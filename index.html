Why visit Guernsey

Guernsey is a beautiful island but is part of several islands. These group of islands are called the Channel Islands which contatin: Alderney, Sark, Herm, Guernsey and Jersey. Guernsey is the second largest of the group with a population of 63,544 (as of 2023).
