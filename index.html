Why visit Guernsey

Guernsey is a beautiful island but is part of several islands. These group of islands are called the Channel Islands which contatin: Alderney, Sark, Herm, Guernsey and Jersey. Guernsey is the second largest of the group with a population of 63,544 (as of 2023). I have lived in Guernsey for 8 years and have been able to see and experience what Island lif is like. If you have an intreeast in Island life then please sign up for more information.
